Form II Mudari Verb فَعَّلَ
Meaning 1. To indicate doing something a lot
2. to make the verb transitive		 Notes 1.     For example, tawwafa means “to do a lot of tawaaf”.

2.     عظُمَ means “to be grand” while عظّم means “to praise” (or “to make someone grand”)
